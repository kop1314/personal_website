Backstory

This project is a real-time updated platform for people around the world to share their thoughts about League of Legends, a multiplayer online battle arena video game. I came out the idea and implement this platform with Xun Zhang and Fang Guo back in 2018 when we took the course, CS421 Database System, in University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Check out here for more information about our team. Since I am including this project in my website, I feel better to keep and improve the parts of project I worked on. The front end of the platform is implemented by HTML, Javascript and CSS. To set up communication between browser and database, I use PHP to make a connection to MySQL database via Ajax calls. The platform is currently hosted by InfinityFree, a free web hosting. To get started with the platform, click here or paste the below website address in your favorite browser!

Registration

In order to access the platform, you have to first create a user account. For the email address, a fake email is good enough because there is no confirmation email. But the fake email must be in valid email format, a @ symbol with an email prefix on its left and an email domain on its right (email_prefix@email_domain). An example of a fake email will be example@mail.com where example is the email prefix and mail.com is the email domain. Simply filling out the below form and click REGISTER will send you to the forum page.

You can also login to the forum with the below email and password:
email: new_user@gmail.com
password: new_user
